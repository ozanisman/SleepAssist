SleepAssist

SleepAssist calculates using a 15 minutes average time to fall asleep

and 90 minute long sleep cycles to calculate sleep and wake times.


The times are calculated so that you wake up between sleep cycles

at the desired time, allowing for an easier rise in the morning.


Specify the time you want to sleep/rise in the designated time

box and click go to find the time that best fits your schedule.
